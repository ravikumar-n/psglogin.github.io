"Darwinism of Cryptdosh"

        To improve is to change; to be perfect is to change often. – Winston Churchill

        We realise the truth of this quote only when we are affected by a change - a change as notable as a country enduring the wrath of black money eradication efforts. This episode brought out many positive responses, but it also showed us how unorganized, ill-prepared and vulnerable we are as a society to an unexpected shift from our conventional lives.

        How better could we have dealt with this? Why did the world not be bothered by the suffering of a nation? What is this cryptocurrency that has become the buzzword? Would it have helped with our situation?

        Cryptocurrency is the decentralized reservoir of digital money that ensures the anonymity and security of your monetary transactions. It gives every man the total authority over his funds, and the responsibilities. In a world where everything is fast becoming digital, how can money be far behind? In such case, Security is a matter of utmost importance. Just like how there are two sides to everything, digitalizing life also has two sides. While many people are working on making it a better place, there are some people who try to take advantage of the loopholes and the amateurs.

        This year’s Login provides a platform to understand the concept of cryptocurrency and security in the digital world better through our various events.

LOGIN 2017

History of Login

        LOGIN, the perfect fusion of masterminds, proves its excellence every year with innovation and proactive tapping to fresh young minds. The event took its birth in the year 1990 and is now entering its 28th year of success. Every year, LOGIN brings participants from all over the world.

About Login

        The 28th edition of LOGIN, a celebrated International level technical symposium is brought to you by the proven alliance of two associations, Computer Applications Association and Computational Sciences Association of PSG College of Technology that aspires you to grapple with the current socio – technological environment. Post graduate students pursuing any stream from colleges across the globe shall embark on the competitive and collaborative search for the clever, the creative and the compassionate.

        The two day symposium scheduled to be held on the 23rd and 24th of september 2017 is an amalgamation of events that refine, recognize and reward people with a 'spark'.

        Login strives to create an environment conducive for students to interact with professionals from the industry, in an effort to bridge the gap between the academia and the industry, Login has done one thing consistently - it has always raised to the bar.
